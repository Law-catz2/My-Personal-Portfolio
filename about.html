My story

I grew up in Saudi Arabia. We enjoy a lot of time in our apartment. Although Filipinos were limited here, we constantly adjust to the Arab life. Except my social skills, because I acted aloof to my Filipino peers. I always lived of not knowing facial expressions and the art of small talk. Normal schools are my enemies. They fail me in all these competitions. Rapid moves and baffling instructions. Giving me a hard time. I have failed under the burden of people. Filipinos called me stupid or annoying, but my passions weren't wasted.


After residing in Dumaguete, I found passions away from school. I share it with my ancestors, except a cousin of a rich dad who graduated in Silliman University. They never believed in independence. Because of my percieved weaknesses, Dumaguete challenged me to innovate that succeeded the masses. It was a seemingly impossible task. Hence, I came for help.
